About

My name is Bailee Egan. I am interested in a variety of topics ranging from bioinformatics, web development, and game development.

I am currently an M.S. student currently working under Dr. Etsuko Moriyama at the University of Nebraska-Lincoln.

In my undergrad, I worked in the lab of Dr. Hope Hollocher at the University of Notre Dame, studying variation in the oral and gut microbiomes of long-tailed macaques.

On the side, I enjoy coding for fun. I mainly use R, Python, and Bash for my research. I've used Javascript and HTML to write small applets and tools. I also draw!
